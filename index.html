“Two friends with cameras and a dream — Gray Light Studio was born on the streets of Omalur.”

What started as a shared passion between two friends has grown into one of Omalur’s most trusted creative studios. We bring together complementary skills — one rooted in technical precision, the other in artistic vision — to create work that genuinely moves people.

From our home base in Omalur, Salem, we’ve documented weddings, built brand identities, and captured life’s quiet moments across Tamil Nadu. Every project we take on gets our whole heart.
